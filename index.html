PETS

Pets are tamed or domestic animals which people keep for companionship or pleasure.Pets are important because they help manage the following.

  • Depression
  • Loneliness
  • Triglyceride levels

It is very important to always keep your clean and give your pet a proper veterinary care so as to keep it healthy. You should always provide your pet with good food fresh water cleen bedding and plenty of exercise.

If by any chance you have children it is very important to teach them on how they can interact with the pets. You should always note that children who are six years and bellow should always be supervised while interacting with the pets. It's also very important to always wash your hands after playing with the pets and also very necessary to teach that to your children.
